Faculty Directory: This section provides a list of all faculty members affiliated with the institution. It may include links to individual faculty profiles or pages.

Departments or Academic Units: If the faculty is organized into different departments or academic units, there may be separate sections or tabs for each department. Each department's section may include information about faculty members, courses offered, research areas, and contact information.

Faculty Profiles: This section features individual profiles or pages for each faculty member. These profiles typically include information such as the faculty member's name, title, academic credentials, areas of expertise, research interests, publications, and contact information.
